Private Instruction

Alicia offers 1-1 sessions for clients. The purpose and structure of these sessions will vary according to client need. They can be used as a means of identifying and setting goals or addressing specific difficulties. The precise structure of each session will vary from client to client but the following is a rough outline of what can be offered.

  • Yoga
  • Flexibility
  • Fitness
  • Restorative
  • Diet
  • Stress Management

Yoga

Individual Yoga sessions can vary from gentle flowing practice to intensive physical practice dependent on need. A private lesson is an opportunity to explore asanas and vinyasas in more depth to benefit general practice. The goal for the session will be agreed prior to the start.

Flexibility

This is a major goal area for lots of people training aerial and pole. Whether you want to work on splits or backs or a flexibility routine to work towards a specific move, these sessions are so helpful in figuring out where your area of tightness or weakness may lie. The sessions will be a mixture of strength and flexibility work and may include using props.

Fitness

These sessions will focus more on strength but can also include programming for increasing endurance. For many, increasing core and upper body strength is a key goal but again sessions are tailored to meet need.

Restorative

Addressing imbalances and niggles in the body. These sessions use a mixture of Pilates and Yoga to promote alignment and correct body function. These sessions can be done in conjunction with Gwen Dolan and Leith Treatments. In the even that you are recovering from a specific injury, medical clearance will be required to participate in these sessions.

Diet

Diet is an essential component of a healthy body and mind. Alicia will work with you on maximising your nutritional intake. She will also work on your relationship with food and helping you to listen to and understand your body and its needs.

Stress Management

Modern living can take its toll with demands getting higher all the time. Sessions are designed to look at coping mechanisms and practical strategies for dealing with day to day stressors.
