Stretch Clinics

What is a stretch clinic?

It's a time and space to either work on goals or help to fix problems.

You can come solo or in small groups if you're all working towards the same goal. Appointments start at 60 minutes.

Issues addressed include hypermobility, injury recovery, flexibility and strengthening goals.

  • Injury recovery/prevention
  • Can also help with poor posture and lower back pain
  • FlexiBeast Clinics can help across a range of disciplines to improve performance by working on increasing range of movement and strength around joints
  • Wherever you are in your flexibility journey clinics are a great way to focus on your individual needs

You can either self refer or be referred by Gwen at Leith Treatments.
