What is a Stretch Clinic?

It's a time and space to either work on goals or help to fix problems. These can be around physical goals, stress management/promoting emotional well being or nutrition/redefining your relationship with food.

You can come solo or in small groups if you're all working towards the same goal. Appointments start at 60 minutes.

Why choose a Clinic?

You will get individual attention from a specialist instructor who will work with you to give you the improvements you want in a safe and supportive environment. This is a level of attention not possible in a normal class setting.

What is treated at a Clinic?

A variety of issues can be assessed and addressed in a clinic.

If you have a particular flexibility goal we can assess where you are then work towards your goal safely combining flexibility and strength work to increase range of motion around a joint while keeping it stable.

If you are recovering from injury we can work on bringing you back to full strength. This would be done in conjunction with health professionals, your safety is key.

Alicia specialises in working with hypermobility and helping people to regain some control and to help prevent injury now and degenerative problems in later life. Hypermobility is a complex condition and Alicia will take a holistic approach in helping you to manage and understand it.

Nutrition is often something people struggle to manage, particularly with the pace of modern living. In a clinic you can look at getting the most from food and look at understanding and improving your relationship with food. Unless you are looking to check for an intolerance, Alicia will not be prescriptive in her approach.....this is about a lifetime of wellness.

Stress is something we juggle in modern living. Using a combination of yoga and psychological practices we can look at regaining balance.

Who can come to a Clinic?

In short, anyone! Clinics are suitable for all levels and experiences.

You do not need to be an experienced Yogi or athlete. Sitting at a desk all day can lead to tightness and constriction. This is found across a wide range of professions including beauty therapy and tattooing/piercing.

Alicia does a lot of work with aerialists but works across many disciplines. Weight lifters benefit greatly from increasing range of movement around joints and this often leads to strength gains while drastically reducing the likelihood of injury. Runners are able to open their hips and reduce/eliminate lower back pain.

If you are tired of an unhealthy relationship with food or just want to feel better in yourself, this is the place for you.
