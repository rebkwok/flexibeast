Group Classes

Classes are deliberately small with a maximum of 9 participants so students get maximum space and attention.

  • Yoga
  • FlexiBeast

Yoga

Vinyasa Flow

A physical and strong form of Yoga combining the flow of Ashtanga with the static poses of Iyengar. These classes aim to build strength and flexibility as well as promote alignment and improved posture. Classes are mixed ability and everybody works to their own level with modifications offered to suit individual need.

Restorative

A more gentle form of Yoga combining flowing and static movements. These sessions are designed to promote wellness and balance in body and mind, bringing you back to your centre.

Flexibeast

Alicia combines Yoga with modern techniques such as PNF and partner work in her own form of flexi training affectionately known as the FlexiBeast approach. This aims to promote flexibility by increasing range of movement around joints while strengthening and stabilising. All classes address both strength and stretch. This approach is designed to promote injury prevention. Classes provide a whole-body workout and will run in themes to enable concentrated goal focus at an appropriate pace.

Classes are an hour and run in 6 week blocks, mats are provided.

You don't have to be strong or bendy to start............we'll get there together.

SensibleSplits

Like the name says, these classes focus on working towards splits or if you're there already taking it to the next level. We work to prepare the muscles you need to open up for splits so that you develop a consistent and safe practice with splits which are also strong. The classes combine Yoga with PNF and partner stretches for maximum results. We may also occasionally use props.

HealthyBendyBacks

These classes aim to stabilise the spine, increase range of movement and build strength. Using a combination of Yoga and Pilates with some partner stretching we also work the abdominals making sure the pelvis and shoulders are not missed out. This will also help to improve posture and is useful if you suffer from lower back pain.

HappyHips

This class addresses the whole pelvic girdle using Yoga, Pilates, PNF and partner work to stabilise and open the area. This class is useful if you're working towards a split or if you have difficulty in your lower back. Particularly beneficial for runners and cyclists who often have tightness in this area. Opening up the hips is useful across so many disciplines and feels amazing!
