Workshops

A workshop is a slightly different environment to a class. We are able to go into more depth and to slow things down so you can work on technique and take things at a more steady pace because we have more time (not to say you won't sweat). The main feedback from people who have come to workshops as well as classes is that they leave with a greater understanding and well and truly stretched.

Workshops will generally be 2-3 hours and numbers are strictly limited at 9. This is to ensure that I have time to get round people, put hands on if needed (in the nicest way possible).

Workshop topics can include:

  • Sensible Splits
  • Happy Hips
  • Healthy Bendy Backs
  • To The Core
  • Partner Stretching
  • FlexiBeast for Everyday
  • Realistic Health
  • Stress Management
