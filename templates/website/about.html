About Alicia

Alicia is very interested in the mind/body link. This led her to study in both fields, holding dual qualifications in fitness and psychology. This makes her approach unique as takes what she has learned and applies this to her teaching. Alicia started working as a fitness instructor, then trained in psychology, trained in yoga and has worked in both fields for the last 10 years. In addition to her experience, she is c onstantly studying and developing new techniques. She learns from the best in the world to enhance her own training and therefore her teaching.
